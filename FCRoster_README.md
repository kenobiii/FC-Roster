## What Is FCRoster?

FCRoster.com is a free web app built for everyone who loves football — players, coaches, captains, referees, scouts, and fans alike.

At its core it does three things:

1. **Build lineups** — drag-and-drop tactical pitch builder with 25 formations across 5v5, 7v7, 9v9, and 11v11
2. **Know your squad** — team management, player cards, and a ranked attribute system inspired by football games
3. **Connect with the game** — a community forum where football people talk tactics, share tips, and ask questions

No app to download. Works on any phone or desktop browser. Free to use.

## Getting Started

### Visit the Site
**[https://www.fcroster.com](https://www.fcroster.com)**

### Create Your Account
Click **Sign In** (top right) and choose:
- **Continue with Google** — fastest, one tap
- **Email / Password** — create an account manually

### First-Time Setup — The Player Evaluation
When you sign in for the first time, you'll be walked through a short setup wizard:

| Step | What Happens |
|---|---|
| **1 — Identity** | Pick your avatar, set your display name, choose your role in football |
| **2 — Vibe Check** | Choose one of 5 cards that describes your current level — this pre-fills your stats |
| **3 — Attributes** | Use sliders to distribute 400 points across PAC, SHO, PAS, DRI, DEF, and PHY |
| **4 — Your World** | Country, positions, preferred foot, club name, social links, bio |

> **Just here for the forums?** There's a *"Skip setup →"* link at the bottom of Step 1 — tap it and you're straight in. You can always fill in your profile later.

Your answers are saved once and **never asked again** unless you go to the Profile tab and choose to edit.

## Feature Guide

### ⚽ Builder Tab

The main tactical lineup builder.

**Controls:**
- **Team Name** — give your squad a name
- **Jersey / Pitch colour** — customise with presets or hex colours
- **Format** — switch between 5v5, 7v7, 9v9, 11v11
- **Formation** — 25 formations available (4-4-2, 4-3-3, 4-2-3-1, 3-5-2 and many more)

**The Pitch:**
- Tap any player position to enter a name
- In **Move Positions** mode, drag players to reposition them freely
- Use **Undo Move** and **Reset** to correct mistakes

**Playmaker Menu** (left panel on desktop, collapsible on mobile):

| Section | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Player Movement** | Toggle drag mode to rearrange positions |
| **Play Phases** | Create multiple phases (build, attack, press) and animate between them |
| **Draw on Pitch** | Draw run arrows (green), pass arrows (yellow), drop the ball |
| **Opposition** | Overlay an opposing team's formation for tactical analysis |

> **Play Phases require a free account.** Phase 1 is always available. Adding phases, naming them, and animating the sequence is unlocked on sign-in.

**Roster Panel** (right panel):
- Tap the expand arrow on any player to add jersey number, age, position, goals, assists, appearances, yellow/red cards, preferred foot, and alternate positions

**Export:**
- The **Share** button generates a downloadable PNG image of your current lineup

**Load Team ⚽** *(requires a team)*:
- If you've created or joined a team, this button fills your pitch with your squad members in one tap

### 🏟️ Community Tab

A forum for football discussion, organised by format tag.

**Browsing:**
- Filter posts by tag: All, 11v11, 9v9, 7v7, 5v5, General
- Formation guide posts are pinned at the top of the feed
- Click any post to read the full thread and comments

**Posting** *(requires sign-in):*
- Tap **+ New Post**, write a title and body, choose a tag
- Your display name, avatar, and player class badge appear automatically alongside your post

**Reactions:**
Posts can be reacted to with three types:
- 🤝 **Fair Play** — good contribution
- 🟨 **Yellow Card** — disagree or caution
- 🟥 **Red Card** — flag for review

Each user's ratio of handshakes to cards generates a **Fair Play %** score visible on posts.

### 👤 Profile Tab

Your personal home on FCRoster.

**If signed out:** Shows a sign-in prompt with a list of what you unlock by joining.

**If signed in, you'll see:**

**Player Card** *(for players and coaches)*
- Your class emoji, overall score (0–100), star rating, and live attribute bars
- Top 2 highest stats highlighted
- An **⚡ Update My Stats** button — note: stats can only be updated once every **30 days** to keep things honest

**Player Classes:**

| Emoji | Class | Score Range | Stars |
|---|---|---|---|
| 🌱 | Grass Roots | 0–20 | ★ |
| ⚽ | Rec Player | 21–40 | ★★ |
| 🏟️ | Club / League | 41–60 | ★★★ |
| 📈 | Comp Player | 61–80 | ★★★★ |
| 🌟 | Semi-Pro | 81–95 | ★★★★★ |
| 👑 | Pro | 96–100 | 👑 |

*Referees show as 🦓 · Fans show as 👀 — no stats required.*

**Team Section**
- **No team yet:** Create a new team or join one with an invite code
- **Has a team:** See your squad, your role (Captain 🎖️ / Asst. Captain ⭐ / Player), and the invite code if you're staff
- **Manage Squad:** Edit player roles, positions, jerseys
- **Leave:** Leave the current team

**Squad Stats**
- Aggregated goals, assists, appearances, yellows, and reds from your saved roster (populated from the Builder's Roster panel)

**Account**
- Roster auto-save confirmation
- Sign out

**✏️ Edit Profile** (top right of the identity card):
- Re-opens the full 4-step setup wizard, pre-filled with your current information
- Update anything at any time

## Team Management

FCRoster has a full squad system built in.

**Creating a Team (via Profile tab):**
1. Go to Profile → tap **CREATE TEAM**
2. Name your team, choose a format (5v5–11v11)
3. Share your **invite code** with your players

**Joining a Team:**
1. Go to Profile → tap **JOIN WITH CODE**
2. Enter the invite code your captain shared

**Roles:**
- 🎖️ **Captain** — can manage the squad, assign roles, regenerate the invite code
- ⭐ **Assistant Captain** — can also manage the squad
- ⚽ **Player** — member of the squad

**Manage Squad:**
- Set each player's role, jersey number, and position preference
- Remove players from the squad

6. **Profile tab** — did it feel like *your* space on the app?

7. **Mobile vs Desktop** — which did you use? Were there any layout issues?

8. **Anything missing** — what feature would make you come back regularly?

9. **Anything broken** — any errors, crashes, or things that didn't work as expected?
